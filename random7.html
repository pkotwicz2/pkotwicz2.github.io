Caret Magnifier Bug Reproduction

This page demonstrates the bug where the caret coordinates sent to the Android View system (via requestRectangleOnScreen) are incorrect when the page is scrolled.

Instructions to reproduce:

  1. Enable Accessibility Magnification on your Android device (usually via Triple-tap or Accessibility shortcut).
  2. Open this page in Chrome or WebView.
  3. Scroll down past the grey gradient spacer to find the blue dashed box.
  4. Tap the input field below to focus it.
  5. Type some text to move the caret.
  6. Observe the magnifier window. Instead of centering on the caret, the magnifier will center far below the actual caret position (potentially off-screen) because the Y coordinate is incorrectly offset by the scroll amount (it is document- relative instead of viewport-relative).
