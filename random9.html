Garfield: The Definitive Guide to the Orange Cat

Character Analysis and History

Garfield is a long-running comic strip created by Jim Davis. The main character is Garfield, a cynical, lazy, lasagna-obsessed orange tabby cat.

He lives with his owner, Jon Arbuckle, a socially awkward cartoonist, and Odie, a sweet but unintelligent beagle. Garfield's hatred of Mondays is legendary, as is his endless appetite for Italian food.

Key themes of the Garfield universe include sleeping, eating, tormenting the mailman, kicking Odie off the table, and attempting to ship Nermal, the self-proclaimed world's cutest kitten, to Abu Dhabi. Garfield has inspired beloved cartoons, movies, and video games.
